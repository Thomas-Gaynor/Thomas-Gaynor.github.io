Outside of school and organised activities, I have a passion for; reading, particularly the novels of John Wyndham and Alexandre Dumas; programming, particularly in machine learning and mathematical applications; and am a neophyte convert to the world of digital art (all art on this page, outside of the title-background, is my own).

Coding
